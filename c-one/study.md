# Capital One
## I'll get there, no matter what!

Questions / study of feedback I've received for the technical, behavioral and case-type interviews.

### General

- Talk as much as possible 
- Ask as many questions as you want (just be mindful of the setting you're in)

### Technical 

- It's about designing a solution rather than properly implementing it 
- It's around 4 steps, you're supposed to move along the interviewer.
- (Personal experience) Asking about the problem and more specifically contrains about it will be the key to success.

### Behavioral 

- They might ask "Tell me about an occassion you've changed the status quo"

### Case-Type Interview 

- You should act as the Business Owner 
- Propose a solution after getting proper information
- You *can* ask questions, but you'd rather move into action ASAP
